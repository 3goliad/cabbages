The Ultimaker 2 Extended printer has a build volume of 22.3cm x 22.3cm x 30.5cm and prints 0.4mm diameter filament at a resolution of up to 20 microns.
Displayed below is an image which represents the number of unique forms the Ultimaker 2 Extended can produce. Each pixel in the image can be interpreted as four base 256 numbers, in RGBA order. Also displayed is an image representing a single unique form as an address within the space described by the first image. Given time, this page will display every form that the Ultimaker 2 Extended will ever be able to create.
